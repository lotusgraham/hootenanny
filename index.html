Catawba Camping

This year's site is on the largest piece of natural white water on the Catawba River. That means camping all weekend, and access to tubing. We use a pro sound system, stage, bonfire, and cater to excellent bands. We will be meeting old friends and carrying on all weekend, so bring food and beer! Feel free to bring tents, camping goodies, food, drinks, toys, lights, insturments, boats, floats etc.
Where: Within 40 min of Asheville
Don't hesitate to contact us if you have a band you'd like to book for this year's Hootenanny! Email Us @ hootenannyreunion@gmail.com
